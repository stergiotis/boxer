# Changelog: 2026-06-24 – 2026-07-22 (feature commits)

Compiles the `feat` commits of a four-week window: 245 of 584 commits. Fixes,
refactors, docs, and chores are out of scope — this entry answers "what can the
codebase do now that it couldn't a month ago", not "what changed". The window is
a date range, not a release boundary: it contains
[`v0.0.9`](https://github.com/stergiotis/boxer/releases/tag/v0.0.9) (2026-06-24)
through [`v0.0.14`](https://github.com/stergiotis/boxer/releases/tag/v0.0.14)
(2026-07-19) and ends 58 commits past `v0.0.14`, untagged.

The middle two weeks overlap the [previous entry](./2026-07-02--2026-07-16.md),
which covers 2026-07-02 – 2026-07-16 at full commit-range depth; where both
describe a change, prefer its detail. ADR files 0096 through 0139 were added in
the window; of those, 0110 was folded into 0113, 0120 was withdrawn and its
number later reused, and 0136–0138 were removed again (numbers burned). Where an
ADR is the authority on a decision, follow the link rather than this entry.

## Breaking changes

Among the feature commits, seven carry the conventional `!` marker, all between
2026-07-04 and 2026-07-05 and all tabled with context in the
[previous entry](./2026-07-02--2026-07-16.md#breaking-changes): identity's

## play: the reactive query graph (ADR-0097)

The whole arc of [ADR-0097](../adr/0097-play-reactive-query-graph.md) (accepted)
landed inside the window, slice by slice:

- **Slice 1–2 — panels become nodes.** Acceptance plus the contract/runtime
  (`840c6412`), Timeline as the first `PanelI` (`8bcd937b`), Detail as a
  selection consumer (`e9660518`), Table and Projection as view-of panels
  (`0464417c`), the graph owning the main node (`3d6ecb10`), and a real
  `nodeExecutorI` with an end-to-end runtime proof (`79432663`).
- **Slice 3 — the split graph.** Statement-split with CTE-lift (`acb01fc3`),
  suspending async node lanes (`93506225`), fuse-to-sink on the live Run path
  (`0e6895b1`), generalized node fusion (`066df665`), a Graph view of the split
  (`65d9dbb4`), and Map retiring its bespoke lane to become a node (`b85ee4c6`).
- **Slice 4 — typed channels.** Panels declare typed input channels
  (`2fe0ea1a`), the Timeline bands lane moved onto `nodeLane` as the `chBands`
  channel (`400b32f2`, `98e23e1b`), with channel inventory UI (`a724726a`).
- **Slice 5 — signals.** The signal-store substrate (`be7a91ea`), selection as
  a real signal (`43c75d4f`), bands extent riding `{tl_min}`/`{tl_max}`
  (`a33a2780`), the map raster on the `vp_*` param seam (`1d45d69e`), and
  signal-writing widgets with a main live toggle (`84869b75`).
- **Slice 6 — first-class tabs.** The tab registry (`4398a95c`), its embedder
  read surface (`9dbd0cc9`), per-panel node binding with selection coherence
  (`b22a033e`), and exported editor-delivery ops (`c8d4e101`).

Recursive CTE nodes realized SD9 (`704d2023`), and the reactive surface itself
renders as a layered drawing (`19f2f2ff`).

## play: query lifecycle and provenance

[ADR-0115](../adr/0115-query-observability-data-plane-strategy.md) (accepted)
went from proposal to working surface: `queryrunsd` captures query runs into
`runtime.facts` (`ca22581b`, S1), the History tab reads them back (`04b777b8`,
S2), live progress streams from the in-band headers (`553d3daa`, S3), and
Tier-1 result pinning freezes any result as a table (`8496ef9e`, S4), with
executed queries stamped with the SD7 identity (`3217d1ef`).

Executed statements are canonicalised — `CanonicalizeFull` heads the
pre-execute stage (`dff99894`), declared as a composite with recorded members
(`3c6f261e`, `5c14fa76`, listed in the Passes tab detail `021f271c`). The
[ADR-0108](../adr/0108-keelson-sql-pass-registry.md) pass registry is applied
at both SQL executors (`811e0770`) with late-bound `Factory` entries
(`81daf32d`), and the Preview tab shows the statement as sent (`7aae711e`).

([ADR-0118](../adr/0118-extbin-external-process-chokepoint.md), **proposed**;
`7d30dc0e`), remaining `os/exec` sites migrated and enforced with CS012

(`8158e622`) and the §SD10 capability gate runs in-process and enforces
(`aa21db40`). Package-role classification gained a `Kind` field (`ee9a5818`)

a hard CI gate (`bdebd8d7`).

A repository-analytics suite grew under `gov/repo`: surviving-line ownership
(`f7f8b0ed`) with a commit stream and progress hook (`27051179`), `.mailmap`
identity canonicalization (`de84284e`), truck-factor authorship facts
(`0f392b4b`), change-coupling co-change extraction (`3b6e7a5d`), and
change-entropy line-churn extraction (`7d904d3a`). `callsites` was rebuilt as
a compiler-adjudicated dispatch classifier
([ADR-0107](../adr/0107-callsites-compiler-adjudicated-dispatch-classification.md);
`f4904dcb`). The ADR corpus gained a sub-item axis, its model extracted to
`gov/adrcorpus` (`d72cf3b0`).

## Verification and pushout

A Quint formal model of the pushout exchange protocol (`49ffaca5`) grew to
cover crash-recovery ack-ordering (`aa6ff5c6`), `Unrecord`/`ReplaceApplied`
crash atomicity (`5cbcd7b0`), exchange-convergence liveness under fairness via
TLC (`6446fee5`), and frontier-reconciliation completeness (`55a2c70e`,
ADR-0079 OQ-1), plus an erasure-vault model (`dbeffa15`). The versioned
write-through cache got its own Quint model (`5a73bf7e`) and a replay-liveness
TLC pair with recorded Apalache proof status (`d8f261e1`). Pushout itself
gained a durable retention ledger with distributed-operation hardening
(`b24d6e74`).
